About Us

Discover the History of Lipa City

Lipa City, Batangas, is a vibrant destination with a rich cultural heritage shaped by ancient Bornean settlers, as well as Indian and Chinese influences. Its historical landmarks, including the San Sebastian Cathedral and Plaza Independencia, are a testament to its colonial past.

Lipa's lively festivals reflect its deep-rooted religious and cultural traditions. Over the years, the city has blossomed into a hub for arts, culture, and education, with institutions like the Lipa Academy of Sports, Culture, and the Arts (LASCA) nurturing local talent.
